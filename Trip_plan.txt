**City Info - Chopta, Uttarakhand**

**Key Attractions:**

1. Tungnath - The highest temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, surrounded by beautiful greenery.
2. Deoria Tal - A quaint spot perfect for camping and witnessing the reflection of the mighty Chaukhamba peak on its surface.
3. Chandrashila - Snow-covered hills offering stunning views, especially after a trek from Tungnath.
4. Kanchula Korak Musk Deer Sanctuary - Home to the Himalayan musk deer and a variety of flora and fauna.

**Local Customs:**

1. Hospitality and Simplicity - The people of Uttarakhand are known for their warmth and welcoming nature.
2. Local Festivals and Events - Nanda Devi Raj Jat, a grand procession held every 12 years, is a significant event in the region.

**Special Events:**

1. Nanda Devi Raj Jat - A grand procession held every 12 years.
2. Trekking events and festivals - Regular events are organized for adventure seekers.

**Daily Activity Recommendations:**

1. Trekking - Explore the Tungnath and Chandrashila treks for breathtaking views of the Himalayas.
2. Cycling - Enjoy the smooth roads surrounded by lush green meadows and snow-capped peaks.

**Weather Forecast:**

Mid-August in Chopta, Uttarakhand, offers pleasant temperatures and lush greenery due to the monsoon season. 
The weather is expected to be partly cloudy, with scattered showers and thunderstorms during the afternoon.
